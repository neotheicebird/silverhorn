Privacy Policy for Silver Horn

Effective date: March 8, 2026

Summary

Silver Horn does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing occurs locally on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information, usage analytics, device identifiers, crash logs tied to individuals, or any other user data.

How Your Data Is Used

Since Silver Horn does not collect data, there is no personal data to use, share, sell, or monetize.

Data Storage and Transmission

Silver Horn operates locally on your device. The app does not transmit your personal data to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Silver Horn does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or external tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Because no personal data is collected, Silver Horn does not knowingly collect information from children.

Your Privacy Rights

If an app does not collect personal data, rights requests such as access, deletion, or correction generally do not apply to app-held data.

Changes to This Policy

If Silver Horn's privacy practices change in the future, this page will be updated with a new effective date before those changes take effect.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact the developer via the repository issue tracker: github.com/neotheicebird/silverhorn/issues
